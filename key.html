Everything you need to know about the Key Machine



  1. Login to your account. (Make sure you have admin privileges)
  2. Click "ADD USER" on the right side of the screen.
  3. Have the employee fill out a User ID, their First and Last name.
  4. After that, click "Fingerprint Not Enrolled"
  5. Press "YES" to the prompt.
  6. You can ask the employee if they would like to use their right index finger or right thumb. Click the word "INDEX" or "THUMB" under Right Hand.
  7. They will now place their finger on the reader four times. Click "DONE" when completed.
  8. Under the word "ROLE" on the left side of the screen, click the dropdown box and select either Service or Sales depending on the employees position.
  9. Click "SAVE USER" at the bottom right.
  10. Click "YES" to add the employee to the network.
  11. Make sure the role you chose is on each of the different SYSTEM NAMES. You can continue by pressing "ADD USER" at the bottom right again.
  12. A temporary password should pop up on screen. Highlight the password with the mouse and either hit CTRL-C or right click the hightlighted text and press COPY.
  13. You may close the temporary password prompt. At the very top middle of the screen, click "LOG OUT".
  14. Have the employee use their finger to sign in. A window should pop up asking to change their password. Right click the current password box and paste the temporary password you copied earlier. Or left click the box and press CTRL-V to paste it.
  15. Have the employee fill out the other two boxes with a password that they want to use. Then click save. If it asks if you want to update to the network, click "YES".
