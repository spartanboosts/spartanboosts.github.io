Everything you need to know about the Key Machine



  1. Login to ERAccess

  2. Type 77 and press enter

  3. Choose either 1, 2, 3, or 4 depending on the store and press enter. (4 is Honda)

  4. After selecting store, press whichever number is sales and press enter

  5. Type 4006 and press enter

  6. Press V and enter

  7. Type in the last six numbers of the VIN

  8. The stock number will be at the top if it is in the system. Also on this page they display a keytag number in case the keys were dropped in the office. This helps identify the cars they belong to.

  9. After that press E and enter

  10. Repeat steps 6-9 until you have all stock numbers

  11. After you’ve gotten all stock numbers and have finished on step 9, press the end button on the bottom of the screen. You can also type "END" in the Stock # field.

  12. Type 4032 and press enter

  13. Press S and enter. (Caps must be on)

  14. Now type in each stock number, pressing enter after each

  15. After all stocks are entered, keep pressing enter until the cursor is blinking next to “Correct (Y/N)”. Type Y and press enter

  16. Now click the printer button at the top of the screen next to help

  17. You now have labels! Before pulling the labels out of the printer, press Feed/Cut on the printer so you can safely remove the labels while leaving a blank one sticking out of the machine.
